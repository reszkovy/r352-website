# r352 — Conference Strategy

> Filter: **Where do my BUYERS go**, not where my peers go.
>
> **Strategic frame:** r352 sells *structural design ops* to multi-complexity organizations across verticals. Our conference strategy reflects that — we go where COO/CEO/VP-tier decision-makers gather, REGARDLESS of vertical, as long as the *multi-X complexity pattern* is present.
>
> Last updated: 2026-05-17

---

## The fundamental error to avoid

Most design consultants attend **peer conferences** — Awwwards, OFFF, Beyond Tellerrand, Brand New Conference. They come back inspired and broke. **Zero leads.**

We attend **buyer conferences** — where the COOs/CEOs of organizations with multi-location, multi-brand, multi-stakeholder complexity actually gather. We don't need design inspiration. We need **decision-makers in the same room as us for 3 days**.

**Conference verticals we hunt across** (matching our portfolio's horizontal play):
- Multi-location fitness / wellness / health
- Multi-location retail / fashion / F&B
- Multi-location hospitality / entertainment venues
- Real estate development / PropTech
- Multi-product enterprise (insurance, banking, telco)
- Multi-brand holding companies / PE-owned portfolios
- Sports federations / major events

## TIER 1 — MUST GO (highest buyer density, broadest horizontal capture)

### 🏢 MIPIM Cannes *(March, annually)*
- **What it is:** World's largest real estate trade show. 27,000 attendees from 80+ countries. Developers, investors, retail leasing, hospitality operators, urban planners, mayors.
- **Who's there:** Archicom-tier developers, hospitality groups, mixed-use mega-project teams. Multi-investment, multi-location, multi-stakeholder by definition.
- **Why we go:** **Single highest density of multi-complexity buyers anywhere in EU.** Real estate + hospitality + retail all converge here.
- **Cost:** Pass ~€1,800. Travel/hotel Cannes-week: ~€2,000. **Total: ~€4,000.**
- **Strategic note:** Expensive but unmatched ROI. Year 1 priority if budget allows. Pre-book 15+ meetings via app.
- **Speaking opportunity:** Yes — submit panel proposal on "Brand operations across multi-investment real estate portfolios" 8 months ahead.

### 🛍️ NRF Big Show *(NYC, January)*
- **What it is:** National Retail Federation's flagship. 40,000 attendees. Every multi-location retailer in the world represented.
- **Who's there:** Multi-location retail CEOs/COOs, Chief Digital Officers, Heads of Store Ops.
- **Why we go:** Retail is one of your strongest verticals (Kubota, omnichannel patterns). Multi-location = textbook r352 problem.
- **Cost:** Pass €1,000+, travel €1,500+. **Total: ~€3,500.**
- **Strategic note:** Year 2 if budget tight. Year 1 if expanding US/global retail presence aggressively.

### 🏬 ICSC European Retail & Shopping Centre Conference *(varies, EU)*
- **What it is:** International Council of Shopping Centers — EU flagship. Mall operators, retail brand directors, leasing strategy heads.
- **Who's there:** Multi-location retail + property crossover. Decision-makers for entire shopping portfolios.
- **Why we go:** **Sweet spot of retail + real estate** — your two strongest patterns. PL Council also has local events.
- **Cost:** ~€1,200 + travel. **Total: ~€2,000.**

### 🏋️ FIBO Cologne *(April, annually)*
- **What it is:** Biggest fitness trade show in EU. ~150,000 visitors. 1,000+ exhibitors.
- **Who's there:** CEOs/COOs of every major European fitness chain. Zdrofit, Benefit Systems, Calypso, Basic-Fit, McFit — all represented.
- **Why we go:** Direct buyer hunt for your strongest case study vertical (Zdrofit + Benefit).
- **Cost:** Attendee pass ~€60–€200. Travel ~€600. **Total: ~€800.**
- **Speaking opportunity:** YES — submit talk on "Operations Design at Multi-Location Wellness Scale" 6 months ahead.

## TIER 2 — HIGH ROI, STRATEGIC FIT

### 🇵🇱 Forum Ekonomiczne Karpacz *(September, Poland)*
- **What it is:** Biggest CEE business conference. 5,000+ attendees. Top Polish + CEE CEOs from every sector — banking, energy, retail, real estate, telco, manufacturing.
- **Who's there:** Multi-product enterprise CEOs (UNIQA-tier), multi-location operators, holding company executives.
- **Why we go:** **Single highest-density gathering of Polish enterprise decision-makers all year.** Cross-vertical = perfect fit for horizontal play.
- **Cost:** ~3,000–5,000 PLN + Karpacz hotel. **Total: ~7,000–10,000 PLN.**
- **Strategic note:** Polish-language home turf, networking-heavy format, low pitch barrier. **Year 1 priority.**

### 🏨 HD Expo + BDNY *(Hospitality Design — US, Las Vegas + NYC)*
- **What it is:** Premier hospitality design conferences. Hotel groups, F&B chains, multi-location operators.
- **Who's there:** Hotel chain VPs, multi-location restaurant operators, hospitality C-level.
- **Why we go:** Hospitality is a major multi-location vertical you haven't penetrated yet but DiscoBowl proves you can deliver.
- **Cost:** Per-event ~€2,500. Pick one per year.

### 🏗️ PropTech Connect London *(June, UK)*
- **What it is:** PropTech industry's biggest annual event. Real estate developers, contech founders, building tech buyers.
- **Who's there:** Archicom and Caterelo's exact buyer profile — real estate operators looking for digital + operational systems.
- **Why we go:** Caterelo direct relevance + Archicom-style real estate buyers in one room.
- **Cost:** ~£800 + travel. **Total: ~€1,500.**

### 🇪🇺 IHRSA European Congress *(October)*
- **What it is:** EU-specific fitness operators' conference. Smaller (300–500) but denser EU C-level audience.
- **Why we go:** Cheaper than global IHRSA. More EU-aligned. Easier to be visible.
- **Cost:** €600–€1,200 pass. **Total: ~€1,500.**

### 🧠 DesignOps Summit *(NYC + virtual, fall)*
- **What it is:** Design Operations practitioners' conference. Run by Rosenfeld Media.
- **Who's there:** In-house design ops leaders at enterprises. **Your buyers' design teams** — VPs of Design who report to CMOs/COOs.
- **Why we go:** Adjacent — you sell *into* the design ops function. Networking with VPs of Design = warm intros to their COOs.
- **Cost:** ~€1,200 in-person or €600 virtual. **Virtual = highest ROI.**
- **Strategic note:** **Speak here.** They actively seek practitioner talks. Submit "AI-native design operations at multi-complexity scale" 4 months ahead.

## SPEAKING STRATEGY — the highest ROI move

**Speaking at a buyer conference = 100x ROI vs attending it.**

You stop being "another vendor on the floor" and become "the operator who explained how multi-complexity design ops works in 35 minutes". COOs reach out to **you**.

### Talks to develop (and pitch to organizers 6 months ahead):

1. **"The 30% margin you're losing to design ops chaos"** — for FIBO, IHRSA, retail, hospitality conferences. ROI angle.

2. **"Brand consistency across 40 locations: a framework"** — for MIPIM, ICSC, NRF, hospitality. Operations angle.

3. **"Why your in-house design team is burned out — and what to systemize"** — for DesignOps Summit, EuropeActive. People angle.

4. **"AI-native design operations: lessons from 40+ location rollouts and 7 verticals"** — for Reuters Next, Web Summit AI track, TNW, Slush. Tech angle. **Strongest cross-vertical pitch.**

5. **"r3loop: a methodology for predictable design at multi-complexity scale"** — for niche ops conferences. Framework angle.

6. **"Real estate as a multi-stakeholder brand problem"** — for MIPIM, PropTech Connect, Property Forum. Vertical-specific entry pitch.

### Submission protocol

- Pitch 6 months ahead minimum
- Bring **proof artifacts**: r3loop badge, Zdrofit + Caterelo + Archicom case studies
- Position as **operator-speaker**, not as agency-pitchman
- Always link talk back to r3loop methodology — own the category
- **Cross-vertical proof** is your unique speaker angle vs. specialist competitors
